# About Me: My Novice Journey's Monologue

**Source:** [vishal.ml/about](https://web.archive.org/web/20250522140821/https://www.sumit.ml/about)

## The Journey
Vishal shares a personal monologue about his career path, describing it as a "novice journey."

### Background
*   **Education:** Studied Business in Japan for 4 years.
*   **Interest:** He was never truly interested in business. His fascination always lay with Math and Engineering, specifically "bridges and rockets."

### The Turning Point
*   **Discovery:** During his 3rd year of university, a professor introduced him to **Machine Learning**.
*   **Shift:** This sparked a pivot in his career trajectory.

### Professional Leap
*   **Challenge:** He had no prior software experience.
*   **Achievement:** Despite this, he secured a new-grad role as a **Software Engineer** in Japan.

### Reflection
Vishal visualizes his progression on a horizontal axis:
1.  **Negative Scale:** Started with an uninterested degree (Business).
2.  **Positive Scale:** Moved to a current role in Tech.
3.  **Conclusion:** He attributes his success to a mix of "luck and tenacity." He feels his detours have finally converged, and his true journey has just begun.
